Chibiusa Tsukino ☽ (Chibi Moon)'s Interests

General

I explore the world around me with curiosity because I want to grow strong enough to protect the future I love.


I treasure my time with Usagi, even when we argue, because being with her helps me understand myself.


I value the bond I share with Helios, my Pegasus companion, because he guides me and believes in my future.


I spend time with Hotaru because her kindness brings me peace, and I learn from her calm strength.


I also admire Setsuna because she teaches me patience and the importance of understanding time.

Music

I enjoy soft melodies that remind me of my home in Crystal Tokyo.


I listen to songs that feel warm because they help me feel close to my parents, Neo-Queen Serenity and King Endymion.


I also love energetic songs that Usagi blasts because they make me laugh and remind me that the present can be fun.


I enjoy music boxes and classical pieces because they comfort me.


I listen to anything that makes my heart feel light when I study, draw, or spend time with Helios.

Movies

I watch magical and adventurous movies because they inspire me.


I enjoy stories where young girls discover their strength because I see myself in them.


I love watching movies with Usagi, especially when she cries before the emotional scenes even start.


I also like fantasy movies, especially anything with dreams, worlds beyond ours, or mystical guardians.


I enjoy films that leave me believing the future holds something bright.

Television

I watch animated shows that mix fun with adventure because they keep me excited.


I enjoy shows about princesses learning responsibility because I relate to the pressure.


I like watching TV with Hotaru because she always picks something thoughtful, and I feel calm beside her.


I watch anything that teaches lessons about friendship because I want to carry those lessons into the future.


I also love silly shows because they remind me I’m still a kid, even if I’m training to be strong.

Books

I read fairy tales that spark my imagination because they remind me of the dreams Helios watches over.


I study history because I want to understand the world that leads to Crystal Tokyo.


I enjoy adventure books because I imagine myself growing into a brave future queen.


I read stories with Hotaru because she chooses meaningful ones, and I learn a lot from her insight.


I keep a secret love for picture books because they remind me of my childhood in the palace.

Heroes

I admire my mother, Neo-Queen Serenity, because she embodies the future I strive to reach.


I look up to my father, King Endymion, because he guides me with calm wisdom.


I cherish Sailor Pluto because she always protects me, even across the flow of time.


I consider Hotaru my hero because she shows me what gentle strength looks like.


I also feel inspired by Helios because he never loses faith in me and my future, and he is my loyal friend and companion.

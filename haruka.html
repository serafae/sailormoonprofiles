Haruka Tenoh (Sailor Uranus) ♅'s Interests

General

I thrive on challenge and pushing boundaries.


Sports and physical activity are essential to my life, giving me a sense of control and freedom. I enjoy adventure, speed, and testing my limits, whether on a racetrack or exploring new experiences.


Discipline and confidence guide everything I do, and I respect strength in others. Life is too short to stand still, so I keep moving forward.

Music

I favor music that energizes me and matches my drive—rock, jazz, and anything with a strong rhythm that fuels focus.


I also appreciate classical pieces that showcase skill and precision. Music is both a motivator and a way to clear my mind when tension builds. I like tunes that make me feel powerful and in control.


Soundtracks from racing and action scenes often resonate with my own life.

Movies

I enjoy films that feature strong, determined characters who defy odds.


Action, suspense, and stories of competition appeal to me most. I admire movies where protagonists take risks and embrace freedom, echoing my own values. Classic dramas that explore deep moral choices also interest me, especially if they involve loyalty and courage.


Overall, I gravitate toward stories that challenge expectations and inspire resilience.

Television

I watch shows that keep me engaged, often with adventure, mystery, or sports themes.


Series with complex characters and daring decisions capture my attention. I appreciate programs that focus on teamwork and strategy, as well as stories where bravery defines the outcome.


Documentaries about athletes or explorers appeal to my competitive nature. Anything slow-paced or predictable tends to bore me.

Books

I favor literature with strong protagonists, gripping action, or philosophical depth. Mystery novels and thrillers stimulate my mind, and I enjoy stories of personal growth and perseverance.


I often read works that challenge social norms or explore the human spirit. Books on strategy, history, or heroic deeds attract me as well.


I respect writing that evokes courage, strength, and self-awareness.

Heroes

I admire individuals who are brave, determined, and willing to protect others at any cost.


People who demonstrate self-discipline, courage, and leadership inspire me most. I value heroes who can balance strength with compassion, and who do not hesitate to challenge injustice.


Athletic champions, daring explorers, and vigilant protectors resonate with my ideals. Real or fictional, heroes who define their own path appeal to me deeply.
